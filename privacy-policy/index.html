Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Chroma Studio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our games and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our Roblox games and services, we may collect:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Roblox username and user ID
  • Contact information when you communicate with us
  • Payment information for in-game purchases (processed by Roblox)
  • Survey responses and feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Gameplay data and statistics
  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 5)

1.3 Information from Roblox

We may receive information from Roblox Corporation in accordance with their platform policies, including:

  • Basic account information
  • Purchase history related to our games
  • Age verification status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our games and services
  • Process transactions and deliver virtual items
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Analyze gameplay to enhance user experience
  • Prevent cheating, fraud, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Communicate important updates and announcements

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Service Providers

We may engage third-party companies to assist with:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support services
  • Payment processing (through Roblox)

3.2 For Legal Compliance

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent harm to others

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication procedures

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our partners may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze game performance
  • Deliver targeted advertisements (through Roblox's systems)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but this may affect certain game features.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are designed for a general audience but may be played by children under 13 through the Roblox platform:

  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's information

Roblox provides parental controls that apply to our games through their platform.

7. International Data Transfers

As our services are global, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States where our servers are located. These countries may have different data protection laws.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@chromastudio.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Gameplay data is typically retained for 12 months after account inactivity.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • In-game announcements
  • Our website
  • Email (for registered users)

Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:

Chroma Studio
Attn: Privacy Officer
Email: privacy@chromastudio.com

For Roblox account-related inquiries, please contact Roblox Support as we cannot access or modify Roblox account information directly.
