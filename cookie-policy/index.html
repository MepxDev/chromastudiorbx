1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites or use online services. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

Similar technologies include:

  • Local Storage: Storing data in your browser for longer-term use
  • Session Storage: Temporary data storage during your visit
  • Tracking Pixels: Tiny invisible images used for analytics
  • Device Fingerprinting: Identifying devices based on configuration

2. How We Use Cookies

Chroma Studio uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These are necessary for our website and games to function properly:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your game session Session
auth_token Keeps you logged in 30 days
consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year

2.2 Performance Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with our services:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
perf Tracks game performance metrics 30 days

2.3 Functional Cookies

These enhance your experience by remembering preferences:

  • theme_pref: Remembers your dark/light mode preference
  • language: Stores your selected language
  • volume: Remembers your game volume settings

2.4 Advertising Cookies

These are used by Roblox and our advertising partners to:

  • Deliver relevant ads within Roblox experiences
  • Limit ad frequency
  • Measure ad effectiveness

We don't directly control these cookies - they are managed through Roblox's advertising systems.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We work with third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Roblox: For platform functionality and advertising
  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis
  • Hotjar: For user experience research
  • Cloudflare: For security and performance

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

4. Managing Cookies

You have several options to control cookies:

4.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all or certain cookies
  • Set preferences for different websites

However, blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality.

4.2 Our Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our website, you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

You can change these settings at any time by clicking the cookie icon in the bottom corner.

4.3 Advertising Opt-Outs

To opt out of personalized ads:

  • Adjust your Roblox ad preferences in your Roblox account settings
  • Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page
  • Use the Your Online Choices tool for European users

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices change or as required by law. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when we last revised this policy.

We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our cookie practices.
