About Us

Crafting the future of Roblox experiences

Founded in 2022, Chroma Studio is a passionate collective of developers, designers, and storytellers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the Roblox platform.

We believe in creating games that aren't just fun, but visually stunning, technically impressive, and deeply engaging experiences that players will remember.

Innovative Gameplay

We design unique mechanics that challenge conventions and deliver fresh experiences.

Stunning Visuals

Our games feature cutting-edge graphics that push Roblox's visual limits.

Player-Focused

Every design decision centers around creating the best experience for our players.
